Swimming

I have been a competitive swimmer for almost 12 years. I started swimming with Swim Neptune when I was 7 years old and continued until colege, where I am continuing to do so with Brandeis University.

Going the Extra Mile

In summer of 2020 during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic I joined a challenge with two other teammates and my coach to swim 25 miles in 24 hours, swimming 1 mile on the hour, every hour. We did this as a fundraising event to help the assisted living homes in our community. Over the duration of this challenge, we raised $36000 to buy personal protection equipment (PPE) for various assisted living locations, and after, we personally delivered all of the PPE.

Water Safety for Refugees

I taught water saftey best practices to refugee children. Many of these kids didn't know how to speak english, so I spent a lot of time trying to overcome this language barrier. Eventually, the kid I was working this learned how to do a basic freestyle and could confidently support himself in the water.
